About

Hi There

Hi, my name is David. I have been passionate about photography ever since I picked up a camera at the age of 13. I still remember that day! Of course I love dogs too. My first dog was a Rough Collie, you remember Lassie? I got her when I was just 6 years old and have many happy memories. I called her Sorla, don't ask me why I gave her that name, I was 6!

Today I have a wonderful dog, Jasper. That's him in the picture with me, a Chocolate Labrador. He is 11 years old now. Such a calm dog, always happy and be anybody's friend, especially when there is food around!

So one day I realised that I can bring my two passions together, photographing dogs. I believe I have an affinity with dogs and this makes a connection when working with a dog. So if you would love to have your dogs picture taken, it would be my pleasure to meet you and your dog and all have a great experience!

David & Jasper
